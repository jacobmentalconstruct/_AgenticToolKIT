How Humans And Agents Share The Loop

The toolbox is strongest when the person brings intent and judgment, and the tooling makes the work legible, verifiable, and easy to resume.

The Shared Loop

1. Human names the need: what matters, what success looks like, and what should stay out of scope.
2. Agent chooses the method: which tool or tier fits, what will be changed, and how it will be checked.
3. Human reviews evidence: tests, smoke checks, docs, screenshots, or manifest-level proof.
4. Agent leaves a handoff: dev log updates, journal surfaces, and a clear next move.

Why The Journal Pattern Still Matters

Even though this repo is itself a toolbox, the journal-centric doctrine still matters because many of the target projects that receive these packages rely on the same park-and-resume rhythm.
